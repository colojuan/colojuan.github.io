About me

 

I’m an efficient graphic designer with experience in UI design, digital design and branding. I’ve worked in a branding consulting agency, co-created a game studio called Emerald Digital Entertainment and created games for mobile phones. I'm currently learning german to get my Master degree in Germany and my dream would be to work there someday.

My time line:

 

  • 1986-1999

    My Humble Beginnings

    Born in Bogotá, raised in Medellín. During this time is where I grew up with Nintendo, SNES and N64 games. My father had a computer store in Medellín and that’s where I played many PC games with my brother Rodrigo when we where kids.

  • 2000-2004

    Life in the USA

    Moved to the United States with my family to learn english. First we moved to Manassas-Virginia and went for 2 years to Manassas Park High School. Then we moved to Clearwater-Florida and I finished my High School diploma at Countryside High School.

  • 2004-2007

    College Experience

    Went to St. Petersburg College to study graphic design. I got my A.S. degree in Print design in 2007 and now it was time to get mi B.A. in graphic design.

  • July 2014

    "Real" graphic designer

    My family and I came back to Medellín to complete my B.A. in Graphic design. I got my degree at Universidad Pontificia Bolivariana in 2014. Since 2013 my brother and I co-created a game group in which we have created 2 games (Meteorz and FSCA). We are working now on new projects and have created an asset for unity.

GameStrap UI

Unity asset to help indie game developers with UI design.

Gamestrap UI is a user interface pack to help indie developers and small companies who don't have time to develop an entire UI skin and want to implement something that looks good in less time. It’s a great tool for fast prototyping.

My role here was to help design an asset that would help designers with no coding experience, easily create an UI in Unity. I helped with the icons, shapes and borders design. I also help to come up with the color schemes.

  • Date Published: March 2015
  • Category: UI Design
  • Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Unity 3D
  • Members: Rodrigo Díaz and Juan Pablo Díaz

Meteorz

Meteorz is a space shooter that combines elements from arcade and casual gaming to bring a new experience to the player.

A quick paced arcade shooter for retro game fans, Meteorz is set in a distant future where mankind’s survival is at peril. Protect the planets from space hazards using missiles, laser beams and over 24 other skills, while you collect enough energy to bring new hope for your people and build a new home.

I was the lead graphic artist, and I was in charge of the entire design of the game. I animated the video and made all the marketing graphics.
